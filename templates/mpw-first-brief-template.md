Do not include GDS, OASIS, netlists, RTL, source code, PDK files, proprietary schematics, proprietary layout, customer rulepacks, or export-controlled technical files in this first brief.

## 8. Files At First Intake

Safe to include:

- This completed first brief
- A public block diagram without proprietary IP
- A one-page public project summary
- Public package or test assumptions

Do not include at public intake:

- GDS or OASIS
- Netlists, RTL, source code, scripts, or proprietary verification files
- PDK files or foundry design-rule files
- Proprietary schematics, layouts, customer rulepacks, or confidential design databases
- Export-controlled technical files

## 9. Questions For MST

- Which process families might fit this request?
- What is missing before partner or fab review?
- What package, wafer-probe, or final-test assumptions should be clarified?
- What NDA or PDK path may be needed?
- Is the request reviewable now, or should the team prepare more information first?

## 10. Requested Response

Choose the response you want first:

- Readiness gap list
- Process-fit questions
- Package / probe / test checklist
- Partner-review packet preparation
- MPW RFQ coordination discussion
- Not sure; ask MST to recommend the next step

## Submission Note

MST uses the first brief to screen readiness, clarify process-fit questions, prepare NDA/PDK and package/test assumptions, and decide whether the case can move toward partner or fab-confirmed review. Availability, pricing, schedule, PDK access, manufacturing route, package, probe, test, and sample delivery scope are confirmed case by case.
